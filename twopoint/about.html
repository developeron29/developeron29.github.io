This isn't a usual introduction page you come across the internet. This is rather something esoteric and we are letting you in, in to the small group of individuals who really gets it.

Your monkey mind might argue that you know who you are and you don't need someone like me to tell you who you are

But, this introduction is of me, why would i want to introduce you to yourself, instead of introducing me? you ask. Because, me and you aren't any different.

and, that is why i don't believe in identities. Never have. Identities are meant to divide us than unite us. If i tell you i am from this nation, have this favourite color. This is only going to divide or unite us depending on, if it matches your opinions and world view. Even if it does unites us, it divides both of us, from a group that doesn't values or isn't in the same group as we.

Manmade boundaries or God-made water valleys cannot separate us. We are the same, always have been. Everybody eats the same food on the same planet, which ultimately transforms into us depending on our genetic constituency. But that doesnot changes the fact that we ate the same apple to become who we are. No matter how we look or what we wear, it's the same apple. You can't deny that.

So i am you and you are me. Same mud. Same air. Same water. Same fire. Same universe. We, as carbon based life forms, are born from this and go back into this, with our identities buried with us, so why have them in the first place? Congratulations, We now have the same thought process to see ourself in unity.
